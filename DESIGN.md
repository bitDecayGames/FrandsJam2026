# Fish Game Design

## Items
- Waders: walk into water up to 3 tiles out from the edge of walkable tiles
- Boat: walk onto any water
- Boots: walk speed +10%
- Sneakers: dash
- Leather shoelaces: dash distance +10%
- Beef Jerkey: increase stamina +10%
- Polarized Glasses: see fish types
- Fishing hat: fish detection +10%
- Gloves: missed cast reel speed +10%
- Steel line: line durability +20%
- Balaclava: cold resistance +20%
- Lock pick: steal 1 fish from another player
- Cork handle: cast distance +10%
- Oil: fish quality chance +10%
